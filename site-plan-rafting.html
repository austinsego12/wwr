Overview

Purpose

Our website will showcase thrilling whitewater rafting trips in Colorado. Its purpose is to attract new customers, provide trip information, and make it easy for families and adventure seekers to book online. The site will also build trust by sharing safety details, guides’ experience, and customer testimonials.

Audience

Our target audience includes outdoor enthusiasts, families, and groups of friends ages 18–55 with moderate disposable income who are looking for fun and memorable vacations. Many are first-time rafters who want a safe but exciting adventure. They usually access the site on mobile devices while traveling, so the site must be simple to navigate, fast-loading, and visually appealing on smaller screens.
